Bonsai Care Tips

Watering

Keep the soil moist but avoid overwatering. Use a spray bottle for precise control.

Pruning

Trim regularly to maintain shape. Use sharp bonsai shears for clean cuts.

Soil

Use well-draining soil like akadama or a lava mix for healthy roots.
